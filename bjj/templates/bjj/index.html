Welcome to Jiujitsuteria

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) is a grappling-based martial art that focuses on ground fighting, submissions, and positional control. It evolved from traditional Japanese jiu-jitsu and judo and was developed in Brazil primarily by the Gracie family in the early 20th century.
